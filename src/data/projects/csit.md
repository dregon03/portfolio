The Canadian Survey on Interprovincial Trade (CSIT) collected data from nearly 30,000 businesses across Canada to understand how goods, services, and workers move between provinces. The survey covers trade volumes, barriers to interprovincial commerce, and challenges with cross-provincial professional certifications and licences.
