Outcome Canada brings together 27 key indicators across seven categories: economic growth, labour market, prices and inflation, housing, trade and business, financial, and demographics. Each indicator shows the latest value, year-over-year change, and reporting period, making it easy to get a snapshot of Canada's economic health at a glance.
